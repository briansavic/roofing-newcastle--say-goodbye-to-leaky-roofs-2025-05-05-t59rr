Mold growth on your roof is more than just an eyesore—it's a serious problem that can compromise the structural integrity of your home and pose health risks to your family. If left untreated, mold can spread rapidly, leading to costly repairs and potential respiratory issues.

Understanding Mold Growth on Roofs

Mold thrives in damp, humid environments, making roofs—especially those with poor ventilation or existing leaks—a prime target. Common causes include:

  • Moisture Accumulation
  • Poor Ventilation
  • Storm Damage
  • Damaged Shingles

The Dangers of Roof Mold

  • Structural Damage
  • Health Risks
  • Decreased Property Value
  • Spread to Other Areas
