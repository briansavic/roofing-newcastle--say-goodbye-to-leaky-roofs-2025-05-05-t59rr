Common Roofing Problems and Eco-Friendly Solutions

1. Expert Roof Repair in Newcastle For Leaks

Leaks are among the most frequent roofing issues, often caused by damaged flashing, cracked tiles, or poor installation. Eco-conscious repairs utilize:

  • Recycled Rubber or Silicone Sealants

  • Green Roofing Systems
