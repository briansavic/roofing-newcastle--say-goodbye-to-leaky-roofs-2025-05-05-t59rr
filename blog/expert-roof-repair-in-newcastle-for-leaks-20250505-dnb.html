Common Roofing Problems in Newcastle

1. Roof Leaks

Leaks can result from:

  • Cracked or missing shingles
  • Damaged flashing around chimneys or vents
  • Poorly sealed roof valleys

2. Damaged Shingles

Professional repair services include:

  • Replacing individual shingles
  • Addressing underlying issues
  • Ensuring proper alignment and sealing

3. Storm Damage

Expert repair services cover:

  • Assessing hail or wind damage
  • Repairing torn or lifted shingles
  • Reinforcing weak spots

Preventative Maintenance Tips

  • Schedule regular inspections

  • Trim overhanging branches
