A well-maintained roof is essential for protecting your home from the elements, ensuring safety, and maintaining energy efficiency. Understanding Shingle Damage

Shingles are the first line of defense against rain, wind, hail, and UV rays. When they are compromised, your home becomes vulnerable to leaks, mold growth, and structural damage. Common signs include:

  • Curling or buckling – Often caused by moisture absorption or poor ventilation
  • Missing shingles – Typically a result of high winds or storm damage
  • Granule loss – Indicates aging or wear and tear
  • Cracks or splits – Can occur due to extreme temperature fluctuations
