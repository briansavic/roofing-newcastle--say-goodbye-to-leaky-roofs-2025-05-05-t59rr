The Importance of Proper Gutter and Roof Maintenance

Gutters play a vital role in directing rainwater away from your roof and foundation. When they become clogged, damaged, or improperly installed, water can pool on your roof, leading to leaks, mold growth, and even structural damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is gutter repair important for my roof?

Gutter repair is crucial because damaged or clogged gutters can lead to water buildup, which may cause roof leaks, structural damage, or foundation issues.

How do I know if my gutters need repair?

Signs include sagging gutters, water overflow, rust or cracks, peeling paint, or water stains on exterior walls.

How often should gutters be inspected?

Gutters should be inspected at least twice a year, ideally in spring and autumn.
