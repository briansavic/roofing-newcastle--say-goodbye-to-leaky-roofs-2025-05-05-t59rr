Expert Roof Repair in Newcastle For Sun Damage

Published on May 5, 2024 • 10 min read

Newcastle's sunny climate, while pleasant for residents, can take a severe toll on roofing materials over time. Prolonged exposure to UV rays, extreme heat, and temperature fluctuations can lead to cracked shingles, warped tiles, and weakened structural integrity. If left unaddressed, sun damage can escalate into leaks, mold growth, and even emergency repairs.

Understanding Sun Damage on Roofs

Sun damage is a gradual but relentless process. Unlike sudden storm damage, UV radiation and heat slowly degrade roofing materials, leading to:

  • Fading and Brittleness: Asphalt shingles lose their protective granules
  • Warping and Curling: Extreme heat can cause material deformation
  • Thermal Expansion: Repeated heating/cooling cycles loosen fasteners
  • Reduced Lifespan: Accelerated aging of roofing materials

Signs Your Roof Needs Sun Damage Repair

Early detection is key to preventing costly repairs. Watch for these warning signs:

  • Discolored or faded shingles
  • Cracked or missing shingles
  • Granule loss in gutters
  • Leaks or water stains
  • Higher energy bills
