Understanding Hail Damage on Roofs

Hailstones vary in size, from small pellets to large chunks of ice, and their impact can cause different types of damage:

  • Granule Loss: Hail can strip away protective granules on asphalt shingles
  • Cracks and Fractures: Shingles may develop cracks
  • Punctures: Larger hailstones can puncture through roofing materials
  • Dented Flashing: Metal components can become damaged
